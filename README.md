# #33 Quick Clip

A hyper-minimalist personal snippet manager to save and copy your most-used text with a single tap.

## Core Philosophy
* **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Save text snippets and copy them to your clipboard instantly.
* **Pure Efficiency**: No searching through notes. Your essential phrases are always one tap away.
* **Private by Design**: Data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. No servers, no tracking.

## How to Use
1. Enter any text (address, email, hashtag, etc.) in the **New Snippet** field.
2. Click **Add Snippet** to save it to your list.
3. **Tap any item** in the list to copy it instantly. The item will flash green to confirm.
4. Use the **×** button to remove snippets you no longer need.

## Features
* **Subtitle Guide**: "Save snippets and copy them with a single tap."
* **Persistent Storage**: Your snippets remain even after closing the browser or restarting your device.
* **Mobile Optimized**: Large, 15px rounded buttons for easy thumb-tapping.

## Privacy & Performance
* No login / No tracking / No ads / Browser-only.
* Ultra-lightweight and fast, even on low-spec devices.
